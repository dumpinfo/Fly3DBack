Gravtor

This effect consists of a sphere that constantly scales down. When it reaches 1/3 of its original size, another one appears with the initial size. Besides, there is a modified particle emmitting system, where a polygon is drawn from the center of the gravtor object to each particle, wrapping a texture over this polygon, making for the feeling of a energy ray (fig. 1).
