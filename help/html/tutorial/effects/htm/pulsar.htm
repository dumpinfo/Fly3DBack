Pulsar

Just a half of the texture is created (fig. 1), and a mirror effect is applied to generate the other half (fig. 2).
Then the dashed line marked on fig. 2 is rotated around itself vertically, and the texture representing the frontal view of the shot is generated (fig. 3). Now the horizontal and vertical textures are positioned like in fig. 4.
* Ps.: The textures are all summed with the back, through a 1-1 blend function, set in the Shader Editor.
